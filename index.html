The ITIN Basic Training Interface website is desinged to facilitate ITIN Tax Examiner (TE) trainig by providing most of the necessary information in one place. During your () week trainig you will be using the Student Basic Training Guide, the Internal Revenue Manual (IRM) and the online Basic Training excercises. In the following sections of this website, General, W-7 Edit and RTS Entry, there are links to the Student Guide and the Internal Revenue Manual. The IRM chapters/references provide rules that a Tax Examiner (you) must follow when editing and entering the Tax Payer information. In the TE Hub section you will find links to other helpful tools you will use as an ITIN Tax Examiner, including Basic Training Excercises.
