RTS Entry

The Internal Revenue Manual (IRM) provides necessary guidance for Form W7 Edit and its Entry into RTS processing software. The ITIN IRM chapters are clearaly and logically organized into a Table of Contents, like in a book. You will find most necessary information using the Table of Contents. Most Form W-7 lines and each Real Time System (RTS) field is guided by several editing/processing rules. Every chapter/paragraph has its own title and a reference number (a nested number such as library/department/book/chapter/paragraph).

If you have trouble finding the necessary information following the Table of Contents, use the word search in SERP. (some IRM paragraphs and references are omitted due to their content not being suited for basic training.)

RTS Screens

The Real Time System (RTS) consists of several screens (pages). Each screen has its own IRM chapter with one to several references, depending on the size and complexity of the screen.
